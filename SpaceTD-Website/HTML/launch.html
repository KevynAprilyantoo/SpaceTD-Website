RECENT SPACE MISSON LAUNCH

Mars Orbiter-3 MISSION


Launch Date: January 8, 2023

Description: The Mars Orbiter-3 Mission represents a significant step in our exploration of the Red Planet. This mission will deploy an advanced orbiter to study Mars' atmosphere, climate, and surface conditions in greater detail. The data collected by the Mars Orbiter-3 will contribute to our understanding of Mars' potential for sustaining future human missions and unlocking the mysteries of the fourth planet from the sun.

StellarSat-1 Deployment


Launch Date: May 15, 2023

Description: The StellarSat-1 Deployment mission is aimed at deploying a cutting-edge communications satellite into orbit. Equipped with advanced technology, StellarSat-1 will enhance global connectivity, providing high-speed internet access to remote areas and facilitating seamless communication across the globe.
